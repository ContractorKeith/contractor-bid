## Included Scope

- 420 LF galvanized chain link fence.
- One 24 FT double swing gate G-1.
- Gate hardware set including latch, hinges, truss rods, and panic hardware shown for G-1.
- Three strands barbed wire where shown on A7.2.

## Excluded / By Others

- Bollards, concrete mow strip, paving restoration, and other civil work.
- Temporary fence, silt fence, and tree protection fence.
- Electrical power, low-voltage conduit, keypad, card reader, and access-control wiring.
